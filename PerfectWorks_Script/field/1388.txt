<Opcode 1 255>'About the excavated
slave generators and
their special effects'.<New>
Due to various studies, it
is a well-known fact that
the output of the 'slave
generators' (hereafter
abbreviated as 'SGs') <-->
strange engines discovered
in structures buried
underground in ancient
times <--> is divided
into three levels.<New>
However, the existence
of a superior mode far
exceeding those has been
newly identified.
For convenience, we shall
refer to it here as
'Hyper Mode'.<New>
Hyper Mode is invoked with
a certain probability once
an SG's Attack Level has
reached 3. The currently
confirmed invocation
probability and conditions,
in the case of an SG
installed in a humanoid
fighting weapon, are
explained below.<New>
In all cases, a basic
constant exists, the
value of which we
approximate to be 5
based on our observations.
The probability is
calculated by multiplying
this constant by one tenth
of the percentage that
HP has dropped.<New>
For example, in a case
where HP has dropped to
50% of its total, then the
invocation probability
would be:
     5 x 50/10 = 25(%)
In other words, once the
Attack Level has reached 3,
in this case there will be
a 25% chance that Hyper
Mode is invoked.<New>
Furthermore, there exist
modifiers besides this,
and by making attacks at
levels 1 to 3 respectively
these modifiers are added
to the base value.
In short, the Hyper Mode
invocation probability
increases the more you
are attacked and the more
you attack.<New>
The SG Hyper Mode, invoked
with the aforementioned
probability and making
it possible to unleash
immeasurable, tremendous
energy for a fixed period,
is surely ideal for the
main engine of a fighting
machine.<New>
But in reality we have not
yet reached the point where
it can be deemed an actual
military strength,
for reasons such as its
unreliability and the fact
that no chassis exists that
is tough enough to even
endure its energy emission.<Close><Close><End of Block>
